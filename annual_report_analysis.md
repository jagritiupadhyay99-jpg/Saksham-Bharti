# Saksham Bharti — Annual Report (2024-25) Analysis & Website Enhancement Plan

This report provides a detailed analysis of the **Saksham Bharti Annual Report (2024-25)** and proposes enhancement opportunities for the website. Since the website is celebrating its **Silver Jubilee (25 Years)**, we can leverage the rich data from the report to add authenticity, impact details, and minor professional touches.

---

## 1. Key Statistics & Impact Metrics

### Data from the PDF
* **Silver Jubilee**: Celebrating 25 years of transformative impact (2000–2025), aiming to scale from "25 to 50 years".
* **Scale of Training**: Over **1,000 youth trained annually** in vocational courses (computers, English, tailoring, beauty culture).
* **Fundraising Independence**: The ecosystem operates **without government funding**, sustained entirely by volunteers and donors.
* **Tax Exemption**: Explicitly states all donations are tax-deductible under **Section 80G** of the Income Tax Act.

### Website Enhancement Ideas
> [!TIP]
> **Home Page / Impact Page Updates**:
> * **Silver Jubilee Badge**: Add a floating or prominent "Silver Jubilee (25 Years) of Empowerment" badge/ribbon on the homepage.
> * **"No Government Funding" Callout**: Highlight in the Donation/Footer section that the NGO is 100% community and volunteer-funded. This increases donor trust.

## 3. Structured Donation & Sponsorship Tiers

### Data from the PDF (Page 41)
The report outlines specific cost breakdowns for sponsoring a student for 6 months:
* **Specialized Training in Computer**: ₹ 5,000/-
* **Stitching & Tailoring**: ₹ 5,000/-
* **Subject Coaching**: ₹ 5,000/-
* **Computer Training**: ₹ 5,000/-
* **Scholarship up to 10th Class**: ₹ 2,500/-
* **Higher Education Scholarship (After 12th)**: ₹ 5,000/-
* **FCRA & Domestic Bank Details**: Explicit account details, SWIFT codes, and IFSC codes are provided.
* **Donor Tokens**: Donors receive a handcrafted token of appreciation created by Saksham Bharti's trainees.

### Website Enhancement Ideas
> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Get Involved / Donate Page Revamp**:
> * **Sponsorship Cards**: Replace general text inputs with clickable cards representing these specific sponsorship tiers (e.g., "Sponsor Stitching & Tailoring for 1 Student: ₹5,000").
> * **Handcrafted Token Callout**: Add a visual message: *"As a gesture of thanks, all donors receive a handcrafted token of appreciation created by our vocational trainees."*
> * **Detailed Bank Layout**: Put a clean, side-by-side tab interface for **Domestic Donations** (SBI) vs. **Foreign Donations (FCRA)** (IOB) with the SWIFT code clearly visible.

## 4. Corporate & NGO Collaborations (Partnerships)

## 5. Key Community & Student Events

### Data from the PDF (Pages 15–30)
The annual calendar includes several high-impact community events:
* **Swami Vivekanand Aajeevika Mahotsav (Mega Job Fair)**: Held at Bhaskaracharya College, Dwarka. 27 Companies, 308 Candidates, 744 Interviews.
* **Quarterly Job Fairs**: Quarterly job drives held across Dwarka, Vikas Nagar, and Raghubir Nagar (390 Selected, 177 Shortlisted).
* **Annual Drawing Competition 2025** (Feb 2, 2025): Held across multiple schools, residential societies, and colleges.
* **Bridging Makeup Artist Inter-center Competition** (March 29, 2025).
* **Ulaas Summer Camps** (May 18–25, 2025): Engaged 450+ children in yoga, Zumba, dance, singing, and art.
* **Karva Chauth Campaign 2024**: Beauty culture and stitching trainees earned **₹45,000/-** by providing henna, nail art, and handmade pouches to the community.
* **International Women's Day** (March 8, 2025): Theme "Strengthening Women, Building Futures" featuring a fashion ramp show by trainees.

### Website Enhancement Ideas
> [!NOTE]
> **Events Timeline / Activities Page**:
> * Organize these into a clean chronological **Annual Highlights Timeline** or a tabbed **"What We Do Beyond Training"** gallery on the Activities page.
> * Showcasing the **Karva Chauth Campaign** and **Aajeevika Mahotsav** shows concrete evidence of student earnings and direct career entry.
